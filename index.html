Want to work with the best?

We’re changing how local businesses communicate with students and growing our efforts to make students’ lives better. We want you to join us. We’re hiring at all levels, both paid and unpaid positions.

Project Managers

Project managers are a hybrid species: a cross between a natural born leader and student culture barometer. Each Project Manager leads a creative team ensuring the delivery of the best work possible. They are a liaison between creatives and clients to guarantee customer satisfaction. A good project manager maintains a crucial understanding of strategy, media planning and client services.

Operations Manager

Day-to-day operations are the steady pulse that keeps our agency alive. The operations manager is a problem solver, diplomat and multitasker. He or she handles internal and external correspondence, scheduling, office management, talent recruitment and event coordinating.

Designers

Good design is more than just good aesthetics. Venture designers use their technical skills and individual style to beautifully encapsulate the project.

Copywriters

The Venture Dept. markets specifically to University of Oregon students. Our copywriters are craftsmen of the English language. They are storytellers who can relate to our audience using humor and well-written prose.

Multimedia Producers

Multimedia producers are storytelling geniuses. They find the story, unique to the prompt, storyboard and shape it to perfection. We expect our producers to be experts in the latest gear and editing software as well as masters over their medium - photo and/or video.

Web Developers

The Venture Dept. is a full-service agency. We offer clients clever solutions to complex digital problems. We need front-end and back-end web developers fluent in HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript.
